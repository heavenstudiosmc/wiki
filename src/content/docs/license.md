# 🔑 License Claiming

To protect our plugins and ensure you receive the best support, we use a licensing system.

## How to claim your license

1. Join our [Discord Server](https://discord.heavenstudios.site).
2. Open a new support ticket in the designated channel.
3. Send a message to claim your license and **attach a screenshot/image of your transaction** (proof of purchase).
4. Our team will review your ticket and provide you with your unique **License Key**.

## How to use the license

1. Open the plugin folder (e.g., `plugins/HeavenRandomKits/`).
2. Open the `config.yml` file.
3. Find the `LICENSE:` field and paste your key:
   ```yaml
   LICENSE: "YOUR-LICENSE-KEY-HERE"
   ```
4. Restart your server. The plugin should now be fully activated!

## Common Issues

- **Invalid IP / IP Limit Exceeded:** If you changed your server host or are testing locally, you might hit the IP limit. Please open a ticket in our Discord to reset your IP limits.
- **Invalid License:** Ensure you didn't copy any extra spaces.
