# 🌌 Introduction

Welcome to the official documentation for **HeavenRandomKits**, an advanced and dynamic kit PvP plugin designed for modern Minecraft servers (Paper 1.20+). 

Unlike traditional KitPvP modes where players manually select their gear, HeavenRandomKits brings chaos and fun to the battlefield: **every time a player respawns, they are assigned a completely random kit.** This forces players to adapt to different playstyles on the fly and ensures that every life is a new experience.

But that's not all. HeavenRandomKits goes far beyond simple kits by introducing dynamic events, economic incentives, and interactive arenas.

---

## ✨ Features Overview

This plugin is packed with systems designed to keep player retention high and gameplay engaging:

* **🎲 Truly Random Kits**: Players get a new kit every time they respawn (fully customizable).
* **🗺️ Rotating Arenas & Voting**: The battlefield changes dynamically! Set up multiple arenas and let your players vote for the next map. FastAsyncWorldEdit (FAWE) automatically loads and pastes the winning arena seamlessly.
* **🛡️ Escape Prevention**: An automatic cage system prevents players from escaping the arena bounds.
* **⚡ 12 Unique Special Abilities**: Enhance combat with items that trigger magical abilities, complete with cooldowns and effects.
* **👑 "Kill The King" Event**: Periodically, a player is crowned the King and given a powerful kit. If someone defeats them, they claim a massive reward!
* **🔥 Killstreaks**: Reward players who survive and conquer. Automatic announcements and rewards at specific kill milestones.
* **💰 Bounties & Internal Economy**: Players earn *Coins* for kills. The system can place automatic bounties on the heads of top players, turning them into high-value targets.
* **🔪 StatTrak Weapons**: Track the exact number of kills on a player's weapon. The lore dynamically updates to show their latest victims!
* **📊 Full PlaceholderAPI Support**: Display stats like highest killstreak, current coins, or active arena on your server's scoreboard.
